Tips of Purchasing a BMW Car

Posted on 2019-02-18 |

In this post I will share some tips learned recently about purchasing a BMW car or SUV.

Of course, it’s a good idea to talk with multiple dealerships to get a sense of the price range.

OL codes

Aside from the usual factory incentives and discounts from dealership (the part that requires negotiation), you may also get additional discount (e.g., $500/$1000) by participating certain BMW events (test drive, holiday celebrations etc.). So it’s a good idea to plan ahead of time if you are interested in purchasing in the future.
